A game in the age of time !

Synopsis :

In a world in crisis, during a period as tough as the lockdown, a few reckless people are still trying to get out for a walk?! Ah these young people, I swear! Understand the real dangers of not respecting the lockdown with Plague Runner !

You will play the only fool in this quiet little town. Running out of pasta, toilet paper or maybe just in need of fresh air you venture outside. But will you manage to survive the dangers of the outside world? Zombies, pangolins, bats...? And don't forget to respect safety distances and to apply barrier gestures!

During your quest you may collect various bonus items that can help you survive! Certificate, hydroalcoholic gel, mask, toilet paper, pasta ...

But what could happen if you become...

infected ?

Finds out by playing...
